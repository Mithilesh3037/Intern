About CartSense

What is CartSense?

CartSense is an AI-powered customer analytics platform built as the final internship project for Module 1–4. It predicts whether a customer is likely to continue purchasing or churn based on their transaction and behavioural data.

The platform integrates a trained Logistic Regression model with a full Flask web application, SQLite database, and a professional SaaS-grade user interface.
